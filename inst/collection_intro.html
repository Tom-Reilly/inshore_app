As an independent coastal State, the UK is responsible for meeting a wide range of international agreements and commitments which involve the collection of fisheries data. In order to meet the Fisheries Act 2020 “scientific evidence objective”, and to ensure the continuity and consistency of fisheries data collection, the Marine Directorate (MD) undertakes data collection as part of the Data Collection Framework.

Below you will find information about the Marine Research Vessels (MRV), Scotia and Alba Na Mara, and the MD market and observer sampling programmes.
